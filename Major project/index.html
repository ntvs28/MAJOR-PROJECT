ABOUT

"DCS" New Digital Craft is combining additive manufacturing and computational design skills to create individually crafted sustainable building components. We add value to our products by keeping design & production in one hand to realize:
Utilizing additive manufacturing, DCS fabricates intricate formworks tailored to individual concrete solutions, enabling the realization of complex shapes and structures. These formworks serve as the foundation for sustainable building processes, promoting efficient material usage and reducing waste.
Moreover, DCS employs advanced computational design algorithms to create digital stones using low carbon concrete. These stones not only offer durability and strength but also significantly decrease the carbon footprint associated with traditional construction materials.
Innovative solutions extend to the creation of non-standard concrete stairs facilitated by bespoke 3D printed formwork. This approach allows for the construction of unconventional staircases, perfectly aligned with the client's architectural vision and functional requirements.
Additionally, DCS specializes in crafting functional shading and light-guiding facade elements using additive manufacturing technologies. These elements not only enhance the aesthetic appeal of buildings but also optimize energy efficiency by controlling light and heat transmission.
Overall, DCS's commitment to sustainability, innovation, and personalized craftsmanship distinguishes them as a leader in the realm of sustainable building component manufacturing.
